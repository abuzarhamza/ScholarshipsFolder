Post Phd/Post Doc Opportunities

We at ScholarshipFolder, trying to build common dedicated database of PhD / Post Doc opportunities to the seeking students and end to end advertising solutions for employee for research projects (directly funded and competition/quota funded); 4 year PhD programmes; research programmes; doctoral training centres, graduate teaching assistantships and international programmes taught in English.

ScholarshipFolder is run by few of the Scholar who tried many times to get into PhD, but never got an admission to acomplish there dream

Belive it. Its True!

Why choose ScholarshipFolder for postgraduate recruitment?

Our aim is to make finding a PhD as simple as possible. All universities include details of their postgraduate studentships on their own web site, but it's impossible for students to visit (and re-visit) every site to see what's available! ScholarshipFolder allows prospective postgraduates to search through 1000s of PhD projects and programmes with just a few clicks.

Large targeted audience

FindAPhD attracts over 180,000 visitors a month* from across the world, and as a dedicated site, our visitors have only one thing in mind - A PhD.

Quick & easy to use

Advertising your PhD has never been more simple; it only takes a few minutes to get started with our online forms. Our team are happy to talk you through each stage of the process, and if you're short on time, we can even do it all for you!
